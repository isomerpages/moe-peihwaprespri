<h1>Parent Volunteers Scheme</h1>
<h5>Parent Volunteer Scheme</h5>
<p>Parents interested in applying for the <strong>Phase 2B Parent Volunteer Scheme</strong>*
for children born between 2nd January 2021 and 1st January 2022 (both dates
inclusive) may submit their application via this link: <a href="https://go.gov.sg/phpps-pv-application" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://go.gov.sg/phpps-pv-application</a> by
the closing date of 31st May 2026. Late submissions will not be considered.</p>
<p>Shortlisted parents may be invited for an interview, and selected parents
will need to serve and complete 40 hours of voluntary service to be eligible
for Phase 2B of the Primary One registration exercise. Please note that
only shortlisted parents will be notified.</p>
<p><strong>Parents should note that this scheme provides eligibility to register at Pei Hwa Presbyterian Primary School under Phase 2B of the Primary One Registration Exercise; it does not guarantee their children a place.</strong>
