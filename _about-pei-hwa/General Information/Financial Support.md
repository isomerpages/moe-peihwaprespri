#### Financial Assistance Scheme (MOE FAS)

To ensure that every student performs at their fullest potential without having to worry about basic needs, MOE Financial Assistance Scheme (MOE FAS) is made available for application throughout the year. Parents can apply for such assistance when the need arises. For more information on the criteria and application of the scheme, pleaseÂ **[click here](https://www.moe.gov.sg/financial-matters/financial-assistance).**

  

Needy students who do not meet the criteria for MOE FAS will be automatically considered for school-based financial assistance scheme.
